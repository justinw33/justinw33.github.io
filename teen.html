Course Information

The course consist of 30 hours of classroom instruction, 6 hours behind the wheel training and 1 hour of behind the wheel observation. In our program, you will learn valuable concepts that include:

  • Fundamentals of Safe Driving
  • Basic Vehicle Control
  • Roadway Markings, Signs, Signals
  • IPDE process
  • Sharing the Road
  • Driving Environments
  • Driving in Adverse Conditions
  • Handling Emergencies
  • Parking

After Completing the Course

Hong Kong Driving School is a Washington state certified and authorized testing location. Here are the steps to obtain your first driver's license:

  1. Must be 16 years old.
  2. Complete the Teenage Drivers Education Program at Hong Kong Driving School.
  3. Have your instruction permit for at least 6 months.
  4. Pass the knowledge test (this step is required for completion of the teenage program).
  5. Pass the skills test.

Additional information can be found on Washington State Department of Licensing's website.
