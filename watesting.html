Hong Kong Driving School is a Washington State authorized testing location. Both the knowledge and skill test can be taken at our location.

Testing for Adults(18 and over)

  1. Pre-apply for your license online through Washington State Department of Licensing's website. Make sure you know your Washington State identification/permit number before the test.
  2. Pass the knowledge test at Hong Kong Driving School.
  3. Pass the skill test at Hong Kong Driving School.
  4. Stop by a Washington State department of Licensing location to pick up your license.

Additional information for adult testing can be found on Washington State Department of Licensing's website.

Testing for Teenagers(16 - 17 years old)

  1. Must be 16 years old.
  2. Complete the Teenage Drivers Education Program at Hong Kong Driving School.
  3. Have your instruction permit for at least 6 months.
  4. Pass the knowledge test (this step is required for completion of the teenage program).
  5. Pass the skill test.

Additional information for teen testing can be found on Washington State Department of Licensing's website.

Preparing for the Knowledge Test

We recommend you to practice here at the Department of Licencing's website to prepare yourself for the knowledge test.
