Steps to Start Your First Lesson

  1. You must be at least 18 years old. If you're not 18 please refer to the teen drivers ed section to start your journey as a new driver.
  2. If you haven't pre-applied for a permit please follow the directions on the Department of Licensing's website. Make sure you know your Washington State permit number, this will be important in future steps.
  3. Take the knowledge test. We recommend you to practice here at the Department of Licencing's website to prepare yourself for the knowledge test.
  4. Once you pass your knowledge test, pick up your instruction permit from a deparment of licensing office. Here is a list of locations.
  5. Schedule your driving lessons at Hong Kong Drivng school by calling (206) 669 - 2822.
